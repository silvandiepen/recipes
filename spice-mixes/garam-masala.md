# Garam Masala

A staple in Indian cuisine, here's my version of it. 

- [ ] 8x    cloves
- [ ] 4x    cumin seeds
- [ ] 2x    star anise
- [ ] 2x    curry leaves  
- [ ] 1x    fennel seeds
- [ ] 1x    black pepper
- [ ] 1x    cardamom
- [ ] 0.5x  black cardamom
- [ ] 0.5x  cinnamon
- [ ] 0.5x  nutmeg
- [ ] 0.25x mace
