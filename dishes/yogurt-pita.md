# Yogurt Pita

### Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup warm water
- [ ] 1 spoon sugar
- [ ] 1 spoon dry yeast
- [ ] 14x full spoon flour
- [ ] 1 spoon salt
- [ ] 1 spoon olive oil
- [ ] 3x full spoon yogurt

### Steps

- [ ] 10 min: add water, sugar and yeast to bowl and let ferment.
- [ ] 15 min: add flour, salt, olive oil and yogurt to bowl and mix.
- [ ] 2 hours: let rise.
- [ ] Separate dough into about 8 portions and shape into flatbreads.
- [ ] Bake until done, not brown, in oven at 180 °C.

When done, you can bake them off in the oven, pan or toaster.
